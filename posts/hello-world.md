# Hello World

Welcome to **Feedbat**, a clean and minimal blog that fetches posts directly from GitHub.

Writing here is simple: create a markdown file, push it, and it appears on the blog. No CMS, no database — just files.

## Why Markdown?

Markdown keeps your content portable, readable in any text editor, and version-controlled with git. It's the simplest possible system for a blog.

Looking forward to writing here more often.
